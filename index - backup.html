SPECIAL STUDIO

Recording studio in Nykarleby since 2018

Special Studio is a recording studio and independent record label based in Nykarleby.
The studio focuses primarily on the sound and style of 1950's Rock N Roll,
with an emphasis on authentic recording techniques.

Unlike many modern studios that rely heavily on digital tools, Special Studio operates with an 8-channel tape recorder and a analog compressors & mixing consoles. We also use real acoustic pianos, drums and tube amps. This approach allows artists to achieve the warm, dynamic sound associated with the golden era of rock, rhythm & blues, and early pop music.

Special Studio works with both local and international artists who share a passion for this type of music.
